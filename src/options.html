Guide & Documentation

Master the Siso Copier workflow.

Core Workflow

  • Sidebar First: Click the extension icon to open the Sidebar. This is your command center.
  • Copy All: One-click to copy the Page Title, Content, and auto-save it to your History.
  • Smart Copy: Use "Copy Page Content" to export just the clean text (no ads/clutter).
  • Reader Mode: Click any saved item in the History tab to open a beautiful, distraction-free Reader View (now with **Images**!).

Smart AI Features

Supercharge your reading with AI. Configure your provider in the Smart AI tab.

  • Summarize Page: Get a concise bulleted summary of the current article.
  • Explain Selection: Highlight any confusing text and ask AI to explain it in simple terms.
  • Privacy First: Your API keys are stored locally on your device (or essentially in a temporary session) and never sent to our servers.

Export & History

  • Rich Markdown Export: Export your history to Markdown with full formatting (headings, links, and **images**).
  • Bulk Management: Delete specific items or "Clear All". (Tip: Use Enter to confirm delete, Esc to cancel).
